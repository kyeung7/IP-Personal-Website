Featured Game: Minecraft (NOW WITH MULTIPLAYER)

Try out the new multiplayer version below! (Requires a good internet connection)

Single player minecraft made in unity. Allows you to change graphics in-game. Full of suprises.
If you have slow connection, leave the tab open for at least 3 minutes. If your connection is good enough,
try out the new multiplayer mode! You can invite up to 9 people to join your game. Just share your invite code.
No permanent saves are available yet. Limited inventory.

Controls:
WASD/Arrow Keys: Movement
Right-Click/Left-Click (Mouse): Build/Break
Up/Down Wheel (Mouse): Scroll through current hotkeys
Mouse/Trackpad: Look around
ESC Key: Open up settings/Exit fullscreen
R/I Key: Open up inventory

Among Us - 3D

3D clone of the game Among US. Compiled with an older version of unity which was missing
language packs. Exiting the game breaks the site. You can only run around as imposter.
When on the main menu, click on "MODO LIBRE." It is a free-roam, multiplayer capabilities
have yet to be implemented. Low end computers may also suffer frame loss.

Controls:
WASD/Arrow Keys: Movement
Right-Click: Kill/Drag
Mouse/Trackpad: Look around
Space Bar: Lock Doors

Try it out for yourself! Click on "MODO LIBRE"
